The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the fourth quarter of 2010 to [Andrew Kuchling](http://www.amk.ca/) in recognition of his work promoting Python everywhere he can.

Andrew chaired PyCon for two years and has contributed significantly to Python's online presence. He also prepares the the ["What's New" documentation](http://docs.python.org/dev/whatsnew/index.html) for new releases of Python.

The Python Software Foundation is pleased to recognize Andrew's contributions to the community.
