The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the third quarter of 2011 to [Arc Riley](http://arcriley.blogspot.com/) for dedicated long-term support through organizing the PSF's [Google Summer of Code](http://socghop.appspot.com/site/home/site) activities, and by managing DNS for PSF servers.

Please join us in recognizing Arc's hard work and dedication.
