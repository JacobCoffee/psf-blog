The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the fourth quarter of 2011 to [Armin Rigo](http://codespeak.net/~arigo/) for his outstanding work on [PyPy](http://www.pypy.org/).

The Python Software Foundation is pleased to recognize Armin's contributions to the community.
