The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the fourth quarter of 2010 to [Doug Hellmann](http://www.doughellmann.com/) in recognition of his contributions to Python online and offline.

Doug is the creator and maintainer of the [Python Module of the Week](http://www.doughellmann.com/PyMOTW/) series, worked on Python Magazine, and is the leader of the PSF Communications team.

The Python Software Foundation is pleased to give this award to this worthy member of the Python community.
