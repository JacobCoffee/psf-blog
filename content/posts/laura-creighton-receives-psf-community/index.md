The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the second quarter of 2011 to [Laura Creighton](http://www.openend.se/) in recognition of her continuous efforts in making community events happen, and especially for her work with the [PyPy](http://pypy.org/) team.

Please join us in thanking Laura for her contributions to the community.
