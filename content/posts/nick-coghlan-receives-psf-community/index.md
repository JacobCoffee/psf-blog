The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the third quarter of 2011 to [Nick Coghlan](http://www.boredomandlaziness.org/) for outstanding core development work and technical leadership, particularly through the [Python Mentors](http://pythonmentors.com/) project.

Please join us in showing Nick that his contributions to the community are appreciated.
