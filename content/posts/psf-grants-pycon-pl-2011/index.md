The Python Software Foundation approved a US$750 grant for the [PyCon PL](http://pl.pycon.org/2011/en/) conference in Poland. This is the fourth edition of the Polish version of PyCon. It aims to integrate developers, designers and managers interested in using Python. Such meeting makes a great opportunity to meet new people and exchange experiences and ideas.

The PyCon PL 2011 was held in Przedwiośnie Hotel situated in Mąchocice Kapitulne near Kielce city from September 22th to September 25th.
