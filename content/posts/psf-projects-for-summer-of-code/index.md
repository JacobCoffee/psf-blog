Google has announced the list of accepted students in the 2007 Summer of Code and the Python Software Foundation will be [mentoring 34 projects](http://code.google.com/soc/psf/about.html). Thank you to everyone who applied and congratulations to the successful students. In addition to the PSF, numerous other mentoring organization are running Python-related projects.

Thanks to [James Tauber](http://www.jtauber.com) for coordinating the selection process for the PSF, and to all of the other mentors who assessed and ranked projects. Greg Wilson has created a page listing [all projects from all participating organizations](http://www.third-bit.com/soc2007.html).
