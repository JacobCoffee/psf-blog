Proposals for [PyCon 2008](http://us.pycon.org/2008/) talks & tutorials are now being accepted. The deadline for proposals is November 16. [PyCon 2008](http://us.pycon.org/2008/) will be held in Chicago, Illinois, USA, from March 13-20. See the [PyCon blog article](http://pycon.blogspot.com/2007/10/call-for-talk-tutorial-proposals.html) for details.
