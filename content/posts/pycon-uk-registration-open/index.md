PyCon UK takes place in Birmingham UK from 12th to 14th September 2008. A full programme includes a day of tutorials; bookings are being taken at [http://www.pyconuk.org/booking.html](http://www.pyconuk.org/booking.html). The early bird rate has been extended to 11th August.

There will be plenary talks by Mark Shuttleworth, Ted Leung and Raymond Hettinger.

You can see a list of already accepted talks at [http://pyconuk.org/talk\_abstracts.html](http://pyconuk.org/talk_abstracts.html) and a schedule for the tutorial day is at [http://pyconuk.org/timetable.html](http://pyconuk.org/timetable.html)
