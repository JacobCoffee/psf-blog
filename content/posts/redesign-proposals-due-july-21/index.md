Just a quick reminder: Proposals for the [python.org](http://python.org/) redesign are due by 11:59 PM EST on July 21, 2012. That's four days away! For more details, please read our [Request for Proposals](http://pythonorg-redesign.readthedocs.org/en/latest/index.html). As always, questions and comments can be emailed to the psf-redesign mailing list at [psf-redesign@python.org](mailto:psf-redesign@python.org).
