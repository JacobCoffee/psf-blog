The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the second quarter of 2011 to [Tarek Ziadé](http://ziade.org/) for his hard work leading the effort to revamp Python's packaging tools.

The Python Software Foundation is pleased to recognize Tarek's contributions to the community.
