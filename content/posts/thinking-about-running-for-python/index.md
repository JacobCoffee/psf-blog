This year’s Board Election Nomination period is [opening tomorrow](https://pyfound.blogspot.com/2023/05/psf-board-election-dates-for-2023.html). Current board members want to share what being on the board is like and are making themselves available to answer all your questions about responsibilities, activities and time commitments via online chat. Please come join us [on Slack](https://www.python.org/join-psf-board-slack) anytime in June to talk with us about being on the PSF board. Board Election Timeline:  

-   Nominations are open, Thursday, June 1st, 2:00 pm UTC
-   Board Director Nomination cut-off: Thursday, June 15, 11:59 pm UTC
-   Voter application cut-off date: Thursday, June 15, 11:59 pm UTC
-   Announce candidates: Friday, June 16th
-   Voting start date: Tuesday, June 20, 12:01 am UTC
-   Voting end date: Friday, June 30, 11:59 pm UTC

Not sure what UTC is for you locally? Check [here](https://savvytime.com/converter/utc)! Nominations will be accepted [here](https://www.python.org/nominations/elections/). (Note: you will need to sign into or create your python.org user account first). Learn more about membership [here](https://www.python.org/psf/membership/) or if you have questions about membership or nominations please email [psf-elections@python.org](mailto:psf-elections@python.org)In addition to [Slack](http://psf-board.slack.com) you are welcome to join the discussion about the PSF Board election on [our forum](https://discuss.python.org/c/python-software-foundation/9).

  
Also, you can see your membership record and status on [psfmember.org](https://psfmember.org/user-information). If you are a voting-eligible member and do not already have a login there, please [sign up](https://psfmember.org/wp-login.php?action=register) and then email [psf-donations@python.org](mailto:psf-donations@python.org) so we can link your membership to your account.
