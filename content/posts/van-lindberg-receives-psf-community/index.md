The Python Software Foundation has presented a [Community Service Award](http://www.python.org/community/awards/psf-awards/) for the first quarter of 2011 to [Van Lindberg](http://www.linkedin.com/in/vanlindberg) for his leadership work on PyCon 2010 and 2011, as well as the pro bono legal work done for the PSF over the years.  
The Python Software Foundation is pleased to recognize Van's contributions to the community.
